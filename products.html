Product Details

Anurav Hydronic Panels include 3- way mixing valves, actuators, pumps, heat exchanger, expansion tanks, glycol auto fill tank, air elimination device, gauges and service valves. Also controller, power disconnect ON/OFF switch and relay - transformer come mounted in a steel framed cabinet. Call Anurav for:

Floor Heating Cabinets

Floor heating cabinets can be used for radiant floor heating applications like classrooms, atriums and homes.

Equipped with either BacNet or stand-alone single and multizone options.

Floor heating cabinets with capacity of 200MBH or 5000 sq-ft area.

Snow Melting Panels

Snow melting panels can be used for parking, ramps, walkways, roof, truck docks and driveway snow melting applications.

Control come with precipitation sensor with stand alone or BAS enable.

Snow melting cabinets up to capacity of 800MBH or up to 4000 sq-ft area.

Hydronic Distribution Panels

Distribution panels can be used for large scale snow melting, floor heating / cooling, make up air and heat pump, electric boiler pump flow stations

Does not include the manifold inside the cabinet.

Distribution panels handle capacity up to 500MBH or up to 3000 sq-ft of area.

Skid Packages

Customized skid packages are completely assembled and skid mounted. Used for large scale snow melting systems, boiler room heat pump packages, circulation assembly, make up air and heat pump, Electric Boiler, etc.

Equipped with BacNet.
